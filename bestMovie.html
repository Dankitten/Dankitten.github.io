Why I love Forrest Gump

The story is a wonderful, motivational one that inspires me. It makes me think about what I'm doing with my life. And, as mama always said, "Life's like a box of chocolates, you never know what you're gonna get."

Favorite Actor

Tom Hanks is a fantastic actor and my favorite in this movie. He perfectly captures the kind of person that Forrest should be.
